KTGauge Bot – Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 12, 2025

1. Data We Collect

We only store these per-guild settings in our database:

  • Guild ID
  • Feedback channel ID
  • Modal title
  • Service choices

No personal user IDs, messages, or other PII is stored.

2. How We Use Data

We use these settings solely to provide the feedback functionality. We do not share data with any third parties.

3. Data Retention

When you remove the bot from your server, your server’s settings are automatically deleted.

4. Your Rights

You may request deletion of any data we hold by removing the bot or contacting us in our support server.
